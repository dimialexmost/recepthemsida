Bacon and Cheddar Avocado Chicken Salad Roll-Ups

Avocado chicken salad with cheddar cheese and bacon, rolled up in tortillas and sliced into snack sized treats.

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 16 minutes
Facts:
  • Calories 93
  • Fat 6g (Saturated 2g, Trans 0)
  • Cholesterol 24mg
  • Sodium 59mg
  • Carbs 2g (Fiber 0.4g, Sugars 0)
  • Protein 6g
  • 3 cups avocado chicken salad
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 (10 inch) tortillas (gluten-free for gluten-free)
  • Tequila

directions:

  1. Buy chicken salad.
  2. Fry the bacon.
  3. Spread the mixture over the tortillas, roll them up.
  4. Slice into 1/2 to 1 inch slices.
  5. Take tequila from the fridge.
